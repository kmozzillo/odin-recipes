Chicken Tikka Masala

Description

This easy recipe makes savory and delicious chicken tikka masala

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds skinless, boneless, chicken thighs
  • 1 Tb vegetable oil
  • 2 Ts kosher salt
  • 2 Ts garam masala
  • 2 Ts ground cumin
  • 1 Ts ground coriander
  • 1 Ts smoked paprika
  • 1 Ts ground turmeric
  • .5 Ts ground black pepper
  • .25 Ts cayenne pepper
  • 1/8 Ts ground cardamom
  • 2 Tb clarified butter(ghee)
  • 1 Onion, chopped
  • .25 cup tomato paste
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 Tb finely grated ginger
  • 1 cup crushed tomatos
  • 13 oz coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup chicken breast
  • 2 Tb chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 Ts red pepper flakes
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Drizzle vegetable oil over chicken in bowl and toss
  2. Whisk together salt, garam masala, cumin, coriander, paprika, turmeric, balck peper, cayenne, cardamom in small bowl. Sprinkle over chicken and coat
  3. Melt clarified butter in a large skillet over high heat, cook chicken thighs until brown, cut into small pieces
  4. Make the sauce, place skillet with chicken dripping over medium-high heat, cook onion until translucent, stir in tomato paste until caramalization, stir ingarlic and ginger
  5. Pour crushed tomatoes into skillet, bring to boil. Pour in coconut milk and chicken broth, bring to a simmer, stir occasionally for 15 minutes
  6. Add chicken and juices, add cilantro and red pepper flakes, simmer and cook for another 10-15 minutes
  7. Season with pepper and salt to taste, and serve hot
